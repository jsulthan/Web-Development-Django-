AI for Automatic Content Generation

"The current automatic content generation method and systems are not able to preserve the context and substance nor can they represent the real significant essence of a body of knowledge. "

Currently human knowledge and the information produced by human in the forms of text, audio, video or multimedia contents are stored in vast repositories of corporate data centers, digital libraries, search engines, and storages of individual computer servers. The only effective tool at the disposal of a knowledge seeker professional for attaining knowledge or information is the service of search engines that provide a great many number of webpages and documents related to a keyword and a subject matter.
